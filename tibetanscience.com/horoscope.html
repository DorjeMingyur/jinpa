Horoscope

Practical Astrology and its uses.

Few most popular predictions chart that people normally tend to get for themselves during the course of lifetime are as follow:

  1. Birth chart( Detailed horoscope)

    Most of the Tibetan people make birth chart soon after baby is born. Because birth chart is like guidance for a people through out his life since it talk about all the major events of your life like health, education, job, financial prospect, marriage, family etc. The aim is not to raise one's anxiety but to avoid unforeseen obstacles with appropriate remedies and precautionary measures. These days, as Buddhist astrology gain is popularity, many westerners crave to get their birth chart done at the age as high as 70. Many of them are able to match their biography with this detailed birth chart!! For the best result, date of birth is not enough but it require exact time of birth.

    In finding the obstacles, you will be suggested with appropriate remedies in the form of prayers and antidotes, which prove to be immensely helpful for many.

  2. Compatibility chart: This is mainly to see how the partner is compatible to each other. Base on the elemental relationship between bride and groom, their general marriage life is predicted. It emphasis on financial status, health, children, fortune and their durability as a partner. Looking at the strength of elements, power balance of the couple is also known. When the relationship between the two is not favorable then different remedies will be prescribed according to Tibetan elemental astrology, to prevent trouble in their marriage life.

  3. One-year prediction: This is done on yearly bases to see what prospects are in store for you in a particular year. Each one of you have your own year- sign and elements, depend on which year you were born. Likewise, a current calendar year is also entitled with its own year-sign and element. Therefore, by calculating the relationship between your own year-sign and the current calendar year, a detailed result and scenario of the particular year is drawn. Appropriate precautionary and remedial measures are then counseled in advance after finding any obstacles or unfavorable things within this particular year.

  4. Medical chart: Medical chart is normally done when an illness does not respond well to a regular treatment. Basically, disease can be of three types. Disease caused by imbalance of humors or elements, disease caused by evil spirit and disease result of your karmic action.
    • Disease caused by imbalance of humors or element can be treat by medicine.
    • Disease caused by evil spirit need to be treated by rituals and antidotes which is why medical chart play some important role. Astrologer will advice antidote to appease and counter these evil spirit. Astrologer can also helps patience to find out which Doctors and system of medicine is favorable for the concern person.
    • Disease due to karmic influence can be avoided by medicine or rituals for a time being but this need to be somehow up root by your good deeds.

  5. Death calculation: In Tibetan community, Astrologer plays a very important role when a person dies. Normally, people immediately consult astrologer for the making of Death chart in which all the detailed procedure are written. It is useful in determining the auspicious date and favorable direction for disposal of dead body. Color of cloths use to cover the death body are also known by using Nine magic square. More importantly, astrologer prescribes certain prayers and rituals not only to make sure the soul of deceased person is rest in peace but also to avoid the effect of certain evil spirit to the family. Survival family will be advice to make an image of certain personal deity on behave of deceased person so that he or she can take a better life in the next birth.
