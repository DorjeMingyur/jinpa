Prayers

Prayers is most significant part of any religion and it is very profound way of submission and devoting to the God. Tibetan Buddhism has its own unique way of relating our self to the god and the spirits in the form of some specific prayers for different purpose. Monk in different monastery normally does these special prayers on behalf of lay people since some of these prayers and mantra are difficult to understand and recite.

This service of performing prayer on your behalf is only for those who do not have access to monastery to get your prayers done. We are going to contact any respected monastery and religious center of your choice in India and Nepal for the especial prayers on your behalf. I hope it will help people in abroad and urban area to fulfill their longing wishes. Original payment bill of the particular monastery will be dispatched towards you or you will be linked to the particular monastery at earliest so that you could verified by yourself.
