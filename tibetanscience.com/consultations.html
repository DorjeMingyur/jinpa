Consultations

Consultation is very effective and helpful for those who don't have enough time to go through the entire detailed horoscope. All the interpretation are done on the basis of your birth chart. Any questions and query related with major event of the personal lives can be asked to the concerned Astro. Practitioner.

Date of birth and time is require in advance. So that the Astrologer can prepare detail chart on which all the narratives are based upon.
