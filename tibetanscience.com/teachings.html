Teaching and Classes

At the time when Tibetan Buddhist astrology is gaining its popularity in the service to humanity, it is important that people should have proper knowledge and understanding so that this art of prediction do not mislead the people for some wrong reasons. I, therefore, committed myself to share information and knowledge with you all of you, being a professional in this fields for a very long time.

It is my utmost duty and aim to preserve and promote Buddhist Astrology with all my sincerity. So, anyone with serious mind are well come to be exposed to and experience the uniqueness of this subject. And I shall make an earnest attempt to connect with the student's mental dispositions for the better understanding.

For those enthusiast students who cannot afford to come here, will be arranged distances learning program through internet. We are very optimistic that learning process with us will be a lifetime experience and highly rewarding for you.

Charges are kept at the minimal rate, in accordance with time we spend on the teaching.
