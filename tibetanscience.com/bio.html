Our Astrologer

Mr. Tenzin Jinpa is a Professional Astrologer from Tibetan Medical & Astro Institute (Men Tsee Khang) of H.H. the Dalai Lama. He was born on 17th feb 1978 at Kollegal Tibetan settlement in South India. He did his primary schooling from C.S.T kollegal. He was a former monk at Sera Jhe monastery where he completed his modern studies along with traditional Tibetan knowledge for more than 10 years. He was then selected for the 6 years intensive course in Tibetan Astrology, Astronomy and medicine at Men Tse Khang in 1997. He has master himself in all the three major field of Tibetan Astro Science viz Elemental astrology of Manjushree linage, Kalachakra system of Buddha Sakyamuni and Arising vowel of Lord Shiva. In 2003 he completed his studies and joined Astro deptt as an Astro practitioner.

He is one of the resource person at Men-Tse-Khang where he has been doing different kind of chart including detailed horoscope, medical chart, compatibility and yearly predictions for his clients all over the world. Beside this, he is taking course in astrology, making almanac & lunar calendar. Almanac and calendar for year 2011 is constructed by Mr. Jinpa as a thesis after completion of his 6 years studies in Astro. Science. He also visited different places in India, Singapore, France and Nepal for conferences, workshop and consultation based on Tibetan Astrology.

After serving 8 year in Astro deptt, he is transferred to dikeyling Tibetan Medical & Astro society to handle newly set-up Astrological center to promote astro science to the larger society in northern India. He is presently working under Tibetan medical & Astro Institute as a senior Astrologer.
